Who we Are
Founded in 2020, The Rhetoric Society of Ghana (RSG) is a multiliteracy organization based in the African country of Ghana. With the help of our members, we strive to grow beyond our previous boundaries across the world. Our goals are to corroborate with other rhetoric societies across the world and grow individually in studying and researching the topics and skills of rhetoric. These goals will be carried out worldwide by our members, but with the heart of the organization located within the beautiful country of Ghana.
