What we believe in
The Rhetoric Society of Ghana (RSG) will provide the country of Ghana an organization where rhetoric can be studied, constructed, and researched. People across Ghana, and the world, will be able to connect on their shared interest in rhetoric and be able to gain knowledge from member collaboration. In addition, RSG will sponsor regular organization meetings and post articles containing research results and collaboration efforts with other rhetoric societies across the world.
About

Who we are

Founded in 2020, The Rhetoric Society of Ghana (RSG) is a multiliteracy organization based in the African country of Ghana. With the help of our members, we strive to grow beyond our previous boundaries across the world.
